# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction

MC Hosting ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our desktop application and related services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- Email address and display name (for account creation)
- Device name (for device registration)
- Server configurations you create

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically
- Device identifier (anonymized)
- Application version
- Connection type (direct vs relay)
- Error codes and crash reports (opt-in only)
- Last online timestamp

### 2.3 Information We DO NOT Collect
- Minecraft world files or server data
- Chat messages or player communications
- Mod or plugin files
- Console logs (stored locally only)
- Payment information (no payments processed)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- Provide and maintain the service
- Coordinate peer-to-peer connections between users
- Send invite codes and manage friend relationships
- Improve the service (opt-in diagnostics only)
- Prevent abuse and enforce rate limits

## 4. Data Storage

- **Your game data** stays on your computer at all times
- **Account data** is stored in our secure cloud infrastructure
- **Connection metadata** is transient and deleted after sessions end
- **Crash reports** are only sent with your explicit consent

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:
- With your consent
- To comply with legal obligations
- To protect our rights and prevent abuse

## 6. Data Retention

- Inactive accounts are automatically deleted after 12 months
- You can request immediate deletion of your account at any time
- Crash reports are automatically deleted after 90 days

## 7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of data collection
- Export your data

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- End-to-end encryption for all connections
- TLS 1.3 for API communications
- JWT authentication with short-lived tokens
- Device-bound session management

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of material changes via the application or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
